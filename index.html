Summary: Latitude vs. X

In this challenge we were to analyze how the weather changes as you get closer to the equator. We first used an OpenWeatherAPI to acquire the dataset on over 500 cities.

After acquiring the dataset, we used Matplotlib to plot different attributes of weather vs latitude. Some of the factors included: temperature, cloudiness, windspeed and humidity.
